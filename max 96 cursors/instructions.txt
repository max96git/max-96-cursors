max 96 cursors - tutorial
1. right click the max 96 installer (.inf) file, and then click "install".
2. go to settings
3. click "themes" or "personalization"
4. find "cursor"
5. Open the advanced cursor settings
6. Click the "Pointers" tab and choose "max 96 design"
7. enjoy
